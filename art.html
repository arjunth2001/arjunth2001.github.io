    • I like to draw, sketch and paint...

    I have been painting, sketching and drawing from a very young age. I find brushes and colours amusing. I have received 10 years of training under a teacher in art such as Sketching, Pencil Shading, Oil Painting , Mural Painting, Clay Modelling etc.

    I have received many prizes and certificates in District Level Competitions for the same.

    Digital Art

    Since 7th grade in School I have been fiddling with Digital Art using Adobe Softwares such as Photoshop, Lightroom, Illustrator. Recently I also started using InDesign, XD, Procreate also for Illustrations. I create all kinds of Digital Illustartions. I occassionally design for websites and the College Magazine , in terms of Magazine Design and Promo Material. I also have some experience with Blender 3D, Autodesk Maya, Mudbox, Zbrush for 3D Modelling and Sculpting.

    The OJ Conundrum Promo: Rick, Morty and Bojack!

    A Rick and Morty meets Bojack Themed Promo I made for The OJ Conundrum Article of College Magazine written by Shashwat Goel. I also designed the article. I made the video using custom puppets made in Illustrator and later animated using Mocap in Character Animator from Adobe. The dialogues, and the animations were later edited in FCPX. Photoshop was also used for some small edits.

    Jekyll and Vandall Comic Book Series

    I illustrated a 4 part Comic as a part of my Course work for Algorithms course with my team. What is a comic without good Illustrations? Each Illustration was hand made and the illustrations saw increase in quality as the episodes progressed to match high levels of standards seen in real comics. The illustrations took a lot of effort and time and were designed with intricate details. They account for more than 100 hours of extreme work. The illustrations were made in Adobe Illustrator, Photoshop and Procreate on iPad. Later they were put together with awesome dialogues in Adobe InDesign. The Illustrations were inspired by our favourite comics and we tried to meet their professional standards. I also designed its Website. Visit it now.

    Adobe CC FCPX

    10 Reasons Why: Ping Promo

    The 10 Reasons Why Series of Instagram Stories were designed and illustrated by me for the college magazine instagram page. The series was inspired by the famous " 13 Reasons Why" Netflix Series where each story is in the form of a TAPE representing and article in the magazine. It can be viewed on the instagram page. The work included use of Illustartor, Photoshop and FCPX
